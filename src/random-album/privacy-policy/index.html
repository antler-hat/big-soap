Privacy Policy for Random Album

Effective date: March 7, 2025

Random Album ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") helps you discover a random album from your saved Spotify library and optionally start playback in Spotify. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how that information is used, and how it is handled.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at bigsoapmusic@gmail.com.

Information the App Accesses

The App accesses the following information in connection with your Spotify account:

  • Spotify authorization tokens used to keep you signed in.
  • Your saved albums library through Spotify's API.
  • Album metadata returned by Spotify, such as album title, artist name, artwork URL, release date, track count, and related Spotify identifiers.
  • Playback-related information needed to send a play request to Spotify when you choose "Play in Spotify."

The App does not request access to your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, precise location, or advertising identifier.

How We Use Information

We use the information above only to operate the App, including to:

  • authenticate you with Spotify;
  • load and cache your saved albums so the App can quickly choose a random album;
  • display album artwork and album details;
  • open the selected album in Spotify; and
  • send a playback request to Spotify if you choose to start playback.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or analytics.

Data Storage on Your Device

The App stores certain information locally on your device:

  • Spotify access and refresh tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain.
  • Token expiration and last sync time are stored in UserDefaults.
  • A cache of your album library is stored in the App's local documents directory so the App can load faster.

This locally stored information is used only to support App functionality.

Information Shared with Third Parties

The App sends information to Spotify only as needed to provide the App's features. For example, the App communicates with Spotify to:

  • complete sign-in and token refresh;
  • fetch your saved albums; and
  • send playback commands when you ask the App to play an album in Spotify.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your information with advertising networks or unrelated third parties.

Spotify is an independent third party, and your use of Spotify is also governed by Spotify's own terms and privacy policy. You can review Spotify's privacy policy at https://www.spotify.com/legal/privacy-policy/.

Data Retention

The App keeps locally stored data for as long as it is needed for the App to function on your device, unless you remove it by deleting the App.

When you log out, the App removes stored Spotify sign-in tokens and related session metadata from the device. Album library data cached on the device may remain until the App is deleted or the cache is replaced by newer cached data.

Security

We use reasonable measures designed to protect information handled by the App, including storing Spotify sign-in tokens in the iOS Keychain. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

Your Choices

You can choose not to connect your Spotify account, although the App will not function without Spotify authentication.

You can also:

  • log out of the App to remove stored Spotify sign-in tokens; and
  • delete the App to remove locally stored App data from your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.
